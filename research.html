| Number Theory

I study the interaction between the geometry of numbers and number theory, from the algorithmic point of view. My work aims at giving sound foundations of an algorithmic Minkovsky theory and develop applications in algebraic number theory, particularly in its Arakelovian context.

| Cryptography

My cryptographical research interests are also related to lattices and thus mostly lie in the so-called lattice-based crypto. My line of work ranges from pure lattice algorithms to implementation related issues such as fault or side-channel attacks on lattice-based schemes.

| Verification

Formal verification is a vibrant field of research, where mathematics and logic are deeply bound to (dis)prove the correctness of programs. I enjoy working on these problematics with tools coming from probability theory, such as couplings or martingales.
